English Dialect Perception Test

Task Overview:

The goal of this task is to evaluate the regional dialect of each speech sample. Please listen carefully to each audio clip and select the corresponding regional dialect. You must be a native speaker of English to participate in this task.

Important Notes:

  • Submissions from non-native English speakers will be rejected and not compensated.
  • To ensure response quality, a few filler samples with clear, undisputed answers have been included. If you fail to answer more than half of these correctly, your work may be rejected and unpaid.
  • If you correctly identify all the filler samples, you will receive an additional bonus reward for your accuracy and effort.

We sincerely appreciate your participation and careful attention!

Instructions

  1. There are a total of 48 speech samples. The task is expected to take approximately 15–20 minutes.
  2. Please complete the test in a quiet environment using headphones for best audio quality and accurate evaluation.
  3. For each sample, you will:
    • Listen to the speech sample
    • Select the regional dialect
    • Rate your confidence in your judgment
    Once both responses are selected, click Next to continue to the following sample.
  4. Before the main test, you will complete a brief trial session to get familiar with the task format.
  5. After completing the task, return to MTurk and submit your confirmation code. This code is generated only once. You may not be compensated if you fail to submit it.
